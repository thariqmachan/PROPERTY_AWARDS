The Venue
The Old War Office, Whitehall, London SW1A 2EU

The Old War Office is a historic building located in Whitehall, Westminster, London, England. It served as the headquarters of the British Army until 1964. The building was originally constructed between 1902 and 1906 and was designed by architect William Young in a neoclassical style.
